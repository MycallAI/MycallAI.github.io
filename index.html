Our Mission

Mycalla Inc. is a for-profit entity whose purpose is to empower and connect the people of Puerto Rico through innovative and reliable communication solutions. We are committed to bridging gaps, fostering collaboration, and enhancing the quality of life for individuals and businesses across the island. Mycalla Inc. aims to be the leading force in transforming communication experiences in Puerto Rico by leveraging cutting-edge technology, fostering local partnerships, and prioritizing customer satisfaction. Our vision is to create a more connected, informed, and prosperous community, driving positive social and economic impact for the people we serve.
